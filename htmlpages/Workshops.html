UPCOMING MUSIC CAMPS AND WORKSHOPS

Throughout the year, School of Tune-Master offers music camps and workshops perfect for musicians of all skill levels who play guitar, bass, drums, keyboard, and vocals.

Explore!

COME TO MUSIC CAMP!

MS-Tunemaster university offer an incredible selection of experiences from beginner music camps to intermediate and advanced sessions. Our music camps often include performance boot camps, artist-themed camps, songwriting and recording, and more—there's a camp for every interest and skill level. Most music camps end with an epic live rock show performed by the campers. Check the School of Tunemaster location closest to you for upcoming music camps and clinics that teach students not only how to play music, but also music theory, songwriting, and Tunemaster 'n' Roll appreciation.

MUSIC CAMPS FOR KIDSAGES 6-9

MS-Tunemaster university camps are scheduled around local schools' vacations and breaks. Our music camps fill your child's day with learning how to play music, working as a team, and more in an awesome music experience.

MUSIC CAMPS FOR TEENSAGES 10-17

MS-Tunemaster university gives teens a music camp experience they'll never forget. In our music camps, students pick up instruments and start playing as a band, ultimately ending the music camp by performing a live show for their friends and families.

MUSIC CAMPS FOR ADULTSAGES 18+

Music camps aren't just for kids. Some School of Tunemaster locations also offer music camps for adults who want a chance to let loose and play music in a band. Adults of every age and skill level are given instruction and rehearsal time, and offered the chance to perform live after every camp session.

FALL MUSIC CAMPS

MS-Tune Master university offers 1-to-2 day themed fall music camps and workshops. In these sessions, kids make huge strides towards taking the stage. These music camps and workshops range from songwriting to performing your favorite classic rock songs.

SPRING MUSIC CAMPS

Spring music camps, kids will learn not only how to play some of their favorite classic rock songs, but also how to perform them with a band on stage. The act of learning how to play songs and perform them live has been proven to provide the ultimate confidence booster, as well as improve their skills as musicians.

SUMMER MUSIC CAMPS

Summer music camps offer students a way to learn how to play songs and, in most cases, perform them live on stage. Kids are placed in bands, have rehearsals and learn more about being true musicians. Summer music camps are offered throughout the season to accommodate school schedules.

WINTER MUSIC CAMPS

Whatever the season, there's MS-Tunemaster university camp for every age and skill level. Our winter music camps and winter workshops offer much more than just learning how to play rock music. Our camps help students form new friendships, and most importantly, they are fun.
