Piano

Piano comes from the original Italian name for the instrument: piano e forte, "soft and loud." Piano is also the musical notation that tells the player that something should be played quietly.

Electric guitars

An electric guitar is a guitar that produces sound by vibrating strings over a pickup that converts the vibrations into electrical signals. Those signals are fed into an amplifier which increases the volume.

Guitars

guitar, Plucked stringed instrument. It normally has six strings, a fretted fingerboard, and a soundbox with a pronounced waist. It probably originated in Spain.

Drums

The Drumset is a set of drums, tom-toms, snare, bass drum, and a series of cymbals, that is commonly found in popular music dating back to the 1920s. It is most commonly played with a pair of drumsticks.
