VOCALS LESSONS

A vocal microphone's job is to accurately mimic the sound of a voice so that it can be boosted and broadcast at volumes that blend in with the other instruments. Some singers, like Lady Gaga, Beyoncé, Ariana Grande, and Justin Bieber, have voices that are large enough naturally for them to perform without a microphone and still sound fantastic. If you're not one of them, then properly using a mic will ensure that you sound good, help you preserve your voice, and give you the chance to improve your voice with reverb, delay, autotune, or other effects.

SONGWRITING LESSONS

One of the most exciting and gratifying activities for musicians is writing original tunes. The School of TUNE MASTER Songwriting Program guides students in the creation of original works by instructing them in the principles of songwriting, arrangement, and music theory. Students receive one weekly private lesson where they learn theory, technique, and ideas on the instrument of their choice. Then, in weekly group courses, these concepts are put into practice as the students collaborate on poetic workshops and songwriting exercises. Students from the Songwriting Program perform on stage or join bands to record their original songs every few months. presently accessible online.
